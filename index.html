Dress Like A Dreamt

We’re starting strong with the quintessential white dress. This inspiration from @maviecottage aka Ana March by using a simple, milk white dress is just the right kind of innocent, subtle charm. It instantly teleports one to the Little Women and the Emma era, making us fit to be on our own book cover! If you’re new to the journey to channel cottagecore vibes in your wardrobe, a simple white dress would be a good place to start. Be mindful of the fabric you choose though, linen and cotton are the better choices.
